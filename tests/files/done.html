Ismaili USA App Now Available for Apple and Android
The Jamati Institutions are excited to launch the Ismaili USA mobile app for Apple and Android devices. This informational app will provide details on Opening Ceremonies, Diamond Jubilee initiatives, announcements, and news. Get up to date information on your regional events, and add them directly to your calendar. The app will be available for download only in the United States.

Time and Knowledge Nazrana (TKN)
TKN is a murid’s gift to the Imam of the Time on the occasion of Diamond Jubilee, and TKN Niyats submitted have no time limit for completion. Jamati members who submitted a TKN Niyat during the Golden Jubilee, whether or not they have been called upon to serve, may still submit a new Niyat for the Diamond Jubilee. Procedure to Register:
  • TKN offerings are currently ongoing. Jamati members wishing to offer their Niyat for the Diamond Jubilee TKN are requested to do so at the designated area in Jamatkhana.
  • Jamati members who have already obtained a TKN registration card are requested to complete the process of creating their profile online by visiting the TKN website at www.timeandknowledge.org
TKN Niyats can be offered until July 2018. For more information, please contact TKN volunteers in Jamatkhana or visit the TKN website.

Have you received the Diamond Jubilee Bulletin? Be sure to add "newsletter@theismailiusa.org" to your safe senders list! All recipients of Ismaili Insight are automatically added to the Diamond Jubilee Bulletin distribution list.
