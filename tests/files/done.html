Time and Knowledge Nazrana (TKN)
TKN is a murid’s gift to the Imam of the Time on the occasion of Diamond Jubilee, and TKN Niyats submitted have no time limit for completion. Jamati members who submitted a TKN Niyat during the Golden Jubilee, whether or not they have been called upon to serve, may still submit a new Niyat for the Diamond Jubilee. Procedure to Register:
  • TKN offerings are currently ongoing. Jamati members wishing to offer their Niyat for the Diamond Jubilee TKN are requested to do so at the designated area in Jamatkhana.
  • Jamati members who have already obtained a TKN registration card are requested to complete the process of creating their profile online by visiting the TKN website at www.timeandknowledge.org
TKN Niyats can be offered until July 2018. For more information, please contact TKN volunteers in Jamatkhana or visit the TKN website.

  ACCESS
The Jamati Institutions provide a range of services to enhance the Jamat’s quality of life. These services include physical and mental health resources, family support services, economic development strategies, education enhancement guidance, and more.

If you have questions about services or resources provided by the Institutions, ask ACCESS. ACCESS is a 24/7 helpline providing support and confidential services to enhance the Jamat’s quality of life. Call ACCESS at 844-55-ACCESS or stop by the ACCESS help desk in the Jamatkhana lobby.

Please see the brochure for more information on ACCESS.

  Need Help Resolving Disputes?
The traditional court system is not always the most effective way to resolve disputes. Lawsuits cost thousands of dollars and are time consuming. The Conciliation and Arbitration Board (CAB) provides confidential mediation and dispute resolutions through a culturally sensitive process at no cost.

CAB helps resolve disputes arising from commercial, business, family, matrimonial, and other civil liability matters. Participation in the process by involved parties is voluntary and strictly confidential. For more information, please contact Chairman Naseem Dhanani Alibhai at (214) 534-0178.

AKYSB Sports Camp
For the first time in any region, AKYSB hosted a Sports Camp for youth in first through sixth grades. With over 100 participants and 35 staff members, including coaches, kids enjoyed learning and practicing skills needed to play basketball and volleyball. They also learned important concepts such as sportsmanship, teamwork, resilience, and goal setting. Camp pictures can be viewed online.

Marking the Diamond Jubilee and 50 Years of Aga Khan Foundation

On July 11, The Aga Khan Foundation (AKF) celebrated the Diamond Jubilee and 60 years since His Highness the Aga Khan assumed the Ismaili Imamat. In the past 60 years the Aga Khan Development Network (AKDN) has become one of the world’s leading development organizations committed to improving the quality of life.

“His Highness has done this with vision, values and inspiring leadership," Michael Kocher, General Manager of AKF, said in a message. "This year will feature efforts to extend further AKDN’s poverty alleviation and other key programs.” AKF is honored to play an integral part in those initiatives.

Learn more about AKF in the United States and its work on its website , and watch a new 2-minute video about AKDN’s life-changing impact.
